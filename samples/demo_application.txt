UNNC Global Engagement Scholarship 2026-2027 — Call for Applications

The University of Nottingham Ningbo China is pleased to announce the Global Engagement Scholarship for the academic year 2026-2027. This scholarship supports outstanding students who demonstrate academic excellence and a commitment to cross-cultural exchange.

Scholarship Value:
- Full tuition fee waiver for one academic year (valued at approximately ¥100,000)
- A living allowance of ¥1,500 per month for 10 months
- One return flight ticket to the student's home country (economy class, up to ¥8,000)
- Priority access to the UNNC Global Leaders Programme

Eligibility Criteria:
1. Currently enrolled as a full-time Year 2 or Year 3 undergraduate student at UNNC
2. Cumulative GPA of 3.5/4.0 or above (or equivalent First Class standing)
3. Active participation in at least TWO cross-cultural or community engagement activities during the current academic year
4. No record of academic misconduct or disciplinary action
5. International students and Chinese students are both eligible
6. Students who have received other full scholarships for the same period are NOT eligible

Required Documents:
1. Completed application form (download from unnc.edu.cn/scholarships/global2026)
2. Personal statement (800-1000 words) describing:
   - Your cross-cultural experiences and their impact on your personal growth
   - How this scholarship will support your academic and career goals
   - Your plan to contribute to the UNNC international community
3. Updated academic transcript (unofficial copy accepted at application stage)
4. Two recommendation letters:
   - One from an academic supervisor or module convenor
   - One from a student organization advisor or community leader
5. Evidence of cross-cultural activities (certificates, photos, or written confirmation from organizers)
6. CV/Resume (max 2 pages)

Application Process:
- Step 1: Submit all documents via the online portal at unnc.edu.cn/scholarships/global2026
- Step 2: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a 15-minute interview (in English) during the week of 5-9 May 2026
- Step 3: Final results announced by email on Friday 23 May 2026

Key Dates:
- Application opens: Monday 31 March 2026
- Application deadline: 23:59, Sunday 20 April 2026
- Shortlist notification: Wednesday 30 April 2026
- Interviews: 5-9 May 2026
- Results announced: Friday 23 May 2026

Selection Criteria:
- Academic performance (40%)
- Quality of personal statement (25%)
- Cross-cultural engagement record (20%)
- Interview performance (15%)

Contact:
For questions, please email scholarships@unnc.edu.cn or visit the Student Financial Support Office (Room PMB-128, Monday-Friday 09:00-17:00).

Note: Incomplete applications will not be considered. Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.
