UNNC International Cultural Festival 2026 – Volunteer & Booth Coordinator Call

Dear Students,

The UNNC International Cultural Festival will be held on Saturday, 12 April 2026, from 10:00 to 18:00 at the Student Square and surrounding areas. This is one of the largest annual events on campus, attracting over 2,000 attendees from the university community and local neighborhoods.

We are looking for:
1. Booth Coordinators (20 positions)
   - Manage a cultural booth representing a country or region
   - Prepare food, games, or cultural displays
   - Each booth team should have 3-5 members
   - Booth setup begins at 08:00 on event day
   - You must attend one mandatory training session:
     Option A: Wednesday 2 April, 18:00-19:30, SA Building Room 201
     Option B: Saturday 5 April, 14:00-15:30, SA Building Room 201

2. Event Volunteers (50 positions)
   - Roles include: registration desk, crowd management, photography, logistics, and cleanup
   - Volunteers must be available for the full day (09:00 – 19:00)
   - A volunteer briefing will be held on Thursday 10 April, 18:00-19:00, location TBA

What You Get:
- CCA hours (5 hours for volunteers, 8 hours for booth coordinators)
- Free festival t-shirt
- Lunch provided on event day
- Certificate of participation
- Great networking opportunity

How to Apply:
- Fill out the application form: forms.unnc.edu.cn/culturalfest2026
- Application deadline: Sunday 30 March 2026, 23:59
- Results will be announced by Wednesday 2 April 2026

Important Notes:
- Priority given to students who have not participated in previous years
- Booth coordinators must submit a booth proposal (theme, activities, budget estimate) along with the application
- All volunteers must attend the briefing session – absence without prior notice may result in removal from the volunteer list
- In case of rain, the event will be moved to the Sports Hall (capacity limited, some booths may need to be consolidated)

Questions? Contact the Cultural Festival Committee at culturalfest@unnc.edu.cn or visit our WeChat group (QR code on the poster outside SA Building).
