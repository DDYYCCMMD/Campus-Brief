COMP2043 Software Engineering — Mid-Term Examination Notice

Dear Students,

This is to inform you of the mid-term examination arrangements for COMP2043 Software Engineering, Semester 2, 2025-2026.

Exam Details:
- Date: Wednesday, 16 April 2026
- Time: 14:00 – 16:00 (2 hours)
- Venue: Sir Peter Mansfield Building, Room PMB-G01 (Ground Floor Lecture Theatre)
- Format: Closed-book, written exam

Exam Structure:
Section A (40 marks): 20 multiple-choice questions covering Weeks 1–6 lecture content
Section B (30 marks): 3 short-answer questions (answer ALL), focusing on UML diagrams, design patterns, and software testing concepts
Section C (30 marks): 1 case-study question (choose 1 out of 2), requiring you to analyze a software project scenario and propose a development plan

Topics Covered:
- Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) models
- Requirements Engineering (use cases, user stories)
- UML: class diagrams, sequence diagrams, activity diagrams
- Design Patterns: Singleton, Observer, Factory, Strategy
- Software Testing: unit testing, integration testing, test-driven development
- Agile & Scrum methodology
- Version control with Git (branching, merging strategies)

Allowed Materials:
- Student ID card (mandatory — no ID, no entry)
- Black or blue pens only (no pencil for final answers)
- A simple non-programmable calculator is permitted for Section B Q3 only
- NO electronic devices, phones, or smartwatches allowed in the exam hall

1. Arrive at least 15 minutes early. Doors close at 14:10 — late arrivals after 14:10 will NOT be admitted.
2. Seating is assigned — check the noticeboard outside PMB-G01 on exam day.
3. You may leave the exam hall after the first 45 minutes, but not in the last 15 minutes.
4. Academic integrity: any form of cheating will result in a mark of zero and referral to the Academic Conduct Committee.
