UNNC 30-Hour AI Hackathon 2026

Date: 28-29 March 2026
Venue: Teaching Building 1, Room TB103 & TB105
Organizer: UNNC Student AI Society & School of Computer Science

Theme: AI for Good – Campus Edition

Tracks:
1. Campus – Build AI tools that improve student life on campus
2. Sustainability – Use AI to promote environmental sustainability
3. Education – Leverage AI to enhance learning experiences

Rules & Guidelines:
- Team size: 3-5 members, cross-faculty teams encouraged
- Each team must have at least one member from a non-CS background
- All coding must be done during the 30-hour hackathon window
- Pre-existing code libraries and open-source tools are allowed, but core logic must be original
- Teams must use at least one AI/ML component (API calls count)
- Each team will receive API credits for MiniMax (details at check-in)

Schedule:
- 28 March, 09:00 – Opening ceremony & team formation
- 28 March, 10:00 – Hacking begins
- 28 March, 18:00 – Dinner & mentor check-in
- 29 March, 08:00 – Breakfast
- 29 March, 12:00 – Code freeze
- 29 March, 13:00 – Presentations begin (5 min pitch + 3 min Q&A per team)
- 29 March, 16:00 – Awards ceremony

Submission Requirements:
- GitHub repository with README (must include: project description, tech stack, how to run, team members)
- 3-slide pitch deck (problem, solution, demo)
- Live demo or recorded demo video (max 2 minutes)
- Submit all materials via the hackathon portal by 12:00, 29 March 2026

Judging Criteria:
- Theme & Topic Relevance: 20%
- Business Potential: 15%
- Product Maturity: 20%
- Depth of AI Application: 20%
- Innovation: 15%
- Technical Implementation: 10%

Prizes:
- 1st Place: ¥5000 + internship interview opportunities
- 2nd Place: ¥3000
- 3rd Place: ¥1500
- Best AI Use Award: ¥1000
- Best Cross-Faculty Team: ¥1000

Contact: hackathon@unnc-ai-society.org
Registration: forms.unnc.edu.cn/hackathon2026 (deadline: 20 March 2026)
