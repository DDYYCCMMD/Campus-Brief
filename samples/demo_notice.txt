IMPORTANT NOTICE: Changes to Semester 2 Teaching Arrangements — Effective 7 April 2026

Dear Students and Staff,

Following the recent campus infrastructure review and feedback from the Student-Staff Liaison Committee, the University has approved several changes to Semester 2 teaching arrangements. Please read carefully and take note of the actions required.

1. TIMETABLE CHANGES (Effective Week 9, starting 7 April 2026)

The following rooms have been reassigned due to the renovation of Teaching Building 3 (TB3):
- All lectures previously in TB3-101 will move to Sir Peter Mansfield Building PMB-LT2
- All seminars previously in TB3-201/202 will move to Science & Engineering Building SEB-305/306
- Labs in TB3-Computer Lab 1 will move to the new Digital Learning Hub (Library Building, Floor 4)

Updated timetables will be published on the UNNC Portal by Friday 4 April 2026. Please check your personal timetable for any changes to your specific modules.

2. ATTENDANCE POLICY UPDATE

Starting from Week 9, the University is implementing a new digital attendance system:
- Students must scan their student ID card at the door reader when entering each class
- The old paper sign-in sheets will no longer be accepted
- If the card reader malfunctions, report to the module convenor immediately — do NOT simply leave
- Students with 3 or more unexplained absences in any module will receive a formal warning letter
- Students with 5 or more unexplained absences may be debarred from the final examination

3. LIBRARY EXTENDED HOURS

To support revision and project work, the Main Library will extend its opening hours:
- Current hours: 08:00 – 22:00 (Mon-Sun)
- New hours (from 7 April): 07:00 – 23:30 (Mon-Fri), 08:00 – 22:00 (Sat-Sun)
- The 24-hour study room (Library Floor 2, Zone C) remains available as usual

4. MODULE EVALUATION SURVEY

The Semester 2 Module Evaluation Survey will open on Monday 7 April and close on Sunday 20 April 2026. All students are strongly encouraged to complete the survey for each module. Your feedback directly influences teaching quality improvements. Access the survey via your UNNC Portal dashboard.

ACTION REQUIRED:
✅ Check your updated timetable on UNNC Portal after 4 April
✅ Ensure your student ID card works with the new door readers — test at the Library entrance before 7 April
✅ Complete the Module Evaluation Survey between 7-20 April
✅ Note the new Library hours if you use evening study sessions

If you have any questions about these changes, please contact the Academic Services Office at academic-services@unnc.edu.cn or visit Room AB-128 during office hours (Mon-Fri, 09:00-12:00 and 14:00-17:00).

Best regards,
Academic Services Office
